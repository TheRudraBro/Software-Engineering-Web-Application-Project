Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Tourism Bangladesh?

Tourism Bangladesh is a platform dedicated to promoting tourism in Bangladesh, providing information about tourist destinations, travel tips, and cultural insights.

How can I book a tour?

You can book a tour through our website by selecting your desired destination and following the booking process. We also offer customer support to assist you with any questions.

What are the payment options?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for your convenience.

Travel Tips

What is the best time to visit Bangladesh?

The best time to visit Bangladesh is during the dry season, from November to April, when the weather is pleasant and ideal for sightseeing.

Do I need a visa to visit Bangladesh?

Visa requirements vary depending on your nationality. Please check with the nearest Bangladeshi embassy or consulate for specific visa information.

Cultural Insights

What are some cultural norms to be aware of?

When visiting Bangladesh, it's important to respect local customs such as dressing modestly, removing shoes before entering homes, and being mindful of religious practices.

What are some must-try foods in Bangladesh?

Some must-try foods include biryani, hilsa fish curry, pitha (rice cakes), and various street foods that offer a taste of Bangladeshi cuisine.
